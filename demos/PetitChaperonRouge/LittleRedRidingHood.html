Fairy tale realization

This is a verbatim reproduction of a well known fairy tale. This shows that jsRealB allows the realization of relatively complex texts. Hovering with the mouse on a sentence displays the jsRealB structure to realize that sentence. The displayed expression is not necessarily identical to the one found in the source that often uses auxiliary functions to factorize the code, while the expression here is rebuilt from informations saved when the function is called.
